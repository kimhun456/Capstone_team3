How would you like to import your passwords?

Select an option below to import passwords to your PasswordBox account.

Import passwords from your browsers

Passwords saved in your Chrome, Firefox or Internet Explorer browsers can be imported directly to your PasswordBox account.

We require a specific version of PasswordBox to complete the browser import. Please complete the four steps below before proceeding. There is a limit of 1000 passwords that can be imported. After logging in to the newly installed PasswordBox extension in your browser, you will be prompted to import your passwords form your browser.

  1. Download the custom version of PasswordBox using the button below.
  2. Close your web browser.
  3. Install the version of PasswordBox you just downloaded.
  4. Log into your PasswordBox account to complete the import.
